Reverie MultiPlug Lamp

User Manual & Features

The Reverie MultiPlug Lamp combines functional elegance with modern convenience. Featuring 2 USB ports, 2 power ports, and a table lamp with 3 selectable light modes — Warm, Cold, and Mix. Designed for your bedside or workspace, it organizes, illuminates, and elevates your environment with timeless style.

2 USB Ports

Charge your devices effortlessly while keeping your space organized.

2 Power Ports

Connect essential electronics with ease without cluttering your desk.

3 Light Modes

Choose Warm, Cold, or Mix to suit your mood and environment.
